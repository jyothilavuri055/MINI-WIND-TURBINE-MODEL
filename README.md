# Mini Wind Turbine Model

## Project Overview
The Mini Wind Turbine Model is a renewable energy project designed to demonstrate the conversion of wind energy into electrical energy. The model showcases the working principles of wind turbines and promotes awareness of sustainable energy solutions.

## Objectives
- Understand wind energy conversion
- Demonstrate renewable energy generation
- Study turbine blade design and efficiency
- Promote eco-friendly power generation

## Features
- Compact and portable design
- Converts wind energy into electrical energy
- Simple and cost-effective model
- Educational and practical application

## Materials Used
- DC Motor/Generator
- Turbine Blades
- LED Bulb
- Supporting Stand
- Connecting Wires

## Working Principle
When wind strikes the turbine blades, the blades rotate and drive the generator. The generator converts mechanical energy into electrical energy, which can be used to power a small load such as an LED.

- Renewable energy education

- Sustainable energy awareness
